Welcome to the UHD Drive Flashing Guide

This guide will help you with the steps to safely flash BP50NB40

Steps for Flashing BP50NB40

  1. Step 1: Download flasher

    Download SDFtool flasher: https://forum.makemkv.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=16&t=22896

  2. Step 2: Download Firmware

    https://github.com/iamjuni/iamjuni.github.io/raw/refs/heads/main/BP50NB40.zip

  3. Step 3: Flashing the drive

    Flash the drive to BP50NB40 (NB50) 1.03-MK.

  4. Step 4: CrossFlash

    Select the RECOVERY option and in the pop up, select the BP50NB40 (NB50) 1.03-MK firmware that you just flashed.

    Select the BU40N 1.00 firmware in the normal drop box.

    Click Start
